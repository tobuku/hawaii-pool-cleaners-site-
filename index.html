Why vinyl pool liners are perfect for island pools

A properly installed vinyl liner gives you a smooth, comfortable surface, clean visual lines, and easier maintenance compared to rough, pitted plaster. When sized and set correctly, it resists wrinkles, reduces algae hangouts, and helps your pool stay brighter, longer.

  • Comfortable underfoot – gentle on keiki feet and hands.
  • Cleaner surface – less porous than plaster, easier to brush.
  • Visual refresh – color and pattern options that transform the whole backyard.
  • Cost-effective – replaces worn surfaces without total reconstruction.

We handle the entire process: measuring, ordering, draining, surface prep, liner install, refill, and chemistry balance—so you can just enjoy the water.

How our vinyl liner replacement process works

1

On-site inspection

We inspect your existing liner, structure, and equipment, measure your pool, and talk through goals, budget, and timing.

2

Custom liner order

Your measurements are sent to the manufacturer for a liner built to your pool’s exact shape and depth profile.

3

Prep & install

We drain, remove the old liner, inspect the floor and walls, make repairs as needed, then carefully hang and vacuum-set the new liner.

4

Refill & balance

We refill, start up the equipment, balance chemicals, and walk you through care so your new liner stays beautiful as long as possible.
