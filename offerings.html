Services

Professional Pool Care & Vinyl Liner Services

From weekly maintenance to full vinyl liner replacements, Hawaii Pool Cleaners provides everything your pool needs to stay clear, safe, and beautiful year-round.

Pool Vacuuming & Weekly Cleaning

Our weekly pool cleaning service keeps your water clear and swim-ready. We use professional-grade vacuums and thorough brushing techniques to prevent algae growth and maintain a pristine swimming environment.

  • Full pool vacuuming
  • Wall and tile brushing
  • Skimming and debris removal
  • Basket emptying
  • Filter pressure checks

Perfect for

  • Weekly or monthly cleaning plans
  • Vacation rentals
  • Busy homeowners
  • New pool owners needing routine support

Chemical Balancing & Chlorine Treatments

Balanced water protects your pool, equipment, and swimmers. We manage chlorine levels, pH, alkalinity, and stabilizers to maintain safe water quality, reduce algae growth, and improve clarity.

  • Chlorine treatment & shocking
  • pH & alkalinity balancing
  • Acid washing (when appropriate)
  • Routine chemistry testing

Benefits

  • Safer swimming conditions
  • Longer equipment life
  • Reduced algae growth
  • Clearer water and better appearance

Vinyl Liner Installation & Replacement

Vinyl liners give your pool a clean, smooth surface and a fresh appearance. Whether you’re replacing an aging liner or upgrading your pool’s look, our installation process ensures a precise, long-lasting fit.

  • Custom measurements for perfect fit
  • Floor and wall inspection
  • Old liner removal
  • New liner install & vacuum set
  • Refill & startup balancing

Why choose vinyl?

  • Smoother and more comfortable
  • Less porous than plaster
  • Easier to maintain
  • More visual options for your pool

Green-to-Clean Pool Recovery

If your pool has gone cloudy or full green, our multi-day recovery process restores clarity without needing to fully drain. We remove debris, treat algae, and rebalance your water to bring it back to life.

  • Shock & floc treatments
  • Multi-stage clarity restoration
  • Vacuum-to-waste when needed
  • Follow-up balancing

Best for

  • Neglected pools
  • Storm debris recovery
  • Homes just purchased
  • Vacation rental turnovers
